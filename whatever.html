  1. Coyote Gulch

    • Permits Available
    • Must be bought at Escalante Visitor's Center or at the Trailhead
    • Permit Price: Free
    • Difficulty: Moderate
    • Length: 2 Nights
    • Distance: Roughly 20 Miles

  2. Zion Narrows

    • Permits Available
    • Permits for the months of April, May, June open March 5th, 10:00 AM MST
    • Permits for the months of July, August, September open April 5th, 10:00 AM MST
    • Permit Price: $27 per person per night
    • Difficulty: Moderate
    • Length: 1 Nights
    • Distance: Roughly 16 Miles

  3. Olympic Coast Trail

    • Permits Available
    • Permits for peak season (May-October) open up April 5th, 7:00 AM PST
    • Permit Price: $14 per person per night
    • Difficulty: Difficult
    • Length: 3 nights
    • Distance: Roughly 16 Miles

  4. Grand Tetons

    • Permits Available
    • Permits open up January 7th, 8:00 AM MST
    • Permit Price: 27 per person per night
    • Difficulty: Difficult
    • Length: Varies
    • Distance: Varies

  5. Half-Dome

    • Permits Available
    • Lottery opens March 1 - March 31
    • Permit Price: $10 per person
    • Difficulty: Moderate
    • Length: 1 Nights
    • Distance: Roughly 14-16 Miles
